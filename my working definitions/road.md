# basic definition:

A road is a transportation way that is primarily used and built for vehicles, essentially they are infrastructure designated with vehicles at forefront of mind though other modes are sometimes allowed to have some space within a section of the road. In OSM roads map to several values due to historical reasons, due to the massive amount of use, it is essentially impossible to see to much change in these values, the most common values are:

 - motorway
 - trunk
 - primary
 - secondary
 - tertiary
 - unclassified
 - residential
 - service
