# basic definition:

Stairs (`highway=steps`) are transportation features mostly intended for foot traffic where due to a difference in incline there are a series of steps formed to alow that difference in height to be more easily navigated. While these are not necessary for a very basic mapping of the pedestrian infra in an area, it is recommended to split stair ways out as quickly as possible as having their positions in a pedestrian network to any amount along with some simple meta tags can greatly enhance the usability for all pedestrians. With stairs the usefulness of a way grows exponentially with the amount of tags placed on it, so the more information provided the better. In some cases where steps are rather spaced out or there are sporadic steps throughout an area `barrier=step` vertices may be placed on `highway=footway` ways, it is highly unadvisable to map stair cases in this manner at the moment. ~
