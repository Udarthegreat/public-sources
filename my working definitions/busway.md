# basic definition:

Sometimes there will be infrastructure built for public service vehicles, these are usually busses in Miami Dade County (and often the rest of the US).Such infrastructure is tagged as `highway=busway` when it is dedicated infrastructure, when A service road within a larger complex (like a bus park and ride) it is tagged as `service=bus` on the service road to indicate that the service road is for use by busses. ~
