# basic definition:

A transportation way is a way that i used to move people or goods from some point A to some other point B (aka it is a way that is built/used by mainly one specific mode of transportation). This is a very general classification and thus there are meany types of transportation ways with even more sub types. There are several main types ofm transportation ways:

 - [roads](/my%20working%20definitions/road.md)
 - [bus infrastructure](/my%20working%20definitions/busway.md)
 - [pedestrian features](/my%20working%20definitions/pedestrian.md)
 - [bicycle infrastructure](/my%20working%20definitions/bicycle.md)
 - hiking infrastructure 
 - tracks
 - rail infrastructure
 - pedestrian streets
 - living streets
 - ~

As mentioned above this roughly maps to `highway=*` but there are others like rail infra that obviously do not fall under that umbrella in the OSM tagging schema. Some ways can carry multiple modes of transport but usually that would mean that the infra is built for one with space allocated for others but one mode is the dominant one on that perticular piece of infra. for example, a road may have a bike lane but roads are still built mainly for cars (vehicles (or at least one type of vehicle)) with the bike lane being a small space allocated for bikes within the road, and they are thus secondary to the vehicles. ~
