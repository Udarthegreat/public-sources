# basic definition:

Some pedestrian infra, while clearly built for pedestrians is not a sidewalk or any other "type" listed elsewhere in the [main pedestrian infra definition](/my%20working%20definitions/pedestrian.md), these types of pedestrian infra are general foot paths and are tagged as `footway=path`. Some examples include paths inside of a park that are not adjacent to parking and footpaths between buildings in a site like a collage/university campus. ~
